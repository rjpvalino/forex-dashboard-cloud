by Richard Valino
⚡ STRATEGY RULES
CONCEPT In a strong weekly trend, price periodically pulls back to the 21 EMA then resumes. We enter at the EMA on the bounce bar — a natural support level with high continuation probability.
ENTRY LONG Weekly ADX ≥ 28 (Trending Up) and daily bar's low touched EMA(21) within 0.5% and closed above EMA(21) and RSI(14) < 82
ENTRY SHORT Weekly ADX ≥ 28 (Trending Down) and daily bar's high touched EMA(21) within 0.5% and closed below EMA(21) and RSI(14) > 18
STOP LOSS 1.5 × ATR(14) from entry — wider SL gives trade room and raises the random-walk WR baseline to 60%
TAKE PROFIT 1.0 × ATR(14) from entry  R:R = 0.67  — quick TP captures the EMA bounce before the trend exhausts
WIN RATE MATH Random-walk WR baseline = SL ÷ (SL + TP) = 1.5 ÷ 2.5 = 60%. Weekly trend filter pushes this above 70%.
MAX HOLD 20 trading bars — exit at market close if SL / TP not hit
RISK / TRADE 1% of current equity per trade  |  Starting equity: $10,000
DATA ~700 daily bars per major pair  |  No look-ahead bias  |  Entry at OPEN of bar after signal
